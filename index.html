About

Vanguard Games was created to focus on making games in a thoughtful, straightforward way. We care about clarity, performance, and building things that feel solid and considered.

The studio is currently in its early stages, with projects being developed and refined internally.

What We’re Working On

Mobile Games

Our current focus is on mobile games. We’re interested in experiences that are easy to pick up, work reliably, and respect the player’s time.

Design decisions are guided by simplicity, usability, and long-term maintainability rather than trends.
